CrazyTeenz

Our youth ministry, CrazyTeenz, prides itself in being a place where students can fall in love with Jesus.

We believe that there is a lack of emphasis on teenagers in the church world today, and we want to go against the trend.

We believe that youngsters can grow up to be the world changers of tomorrow, and it starts with us making an impact today.

CrazyKidz

CrazyKidz is a place that children love to come back to, and is a place where they can be loved.

Your kids will have a blast with all of the fun activities and adventures that will take part every week.

We believe that your kids deserve to be loved and nurtured, so leave it to us and Jesus.

-Crazy Carrol, Childrens Pastor
